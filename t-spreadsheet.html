Organizing spreadsheets

Context

LibreOffice 4.3 Calc can be used to organize efficient spreadsheets.

Prerequisites

  • LibreOffice 4.3

Steps

  1. Open LibreOffice 4.3, and click 'Calc Spreadsheet' to open a new spreadsheet

    To open an existing spreadsheet click 'Open File' instead (Figure 1 Below)

  2. Make your calculations

    Be sure to learn a few of calc's built in functions:

    • LibreOffice Calc Functions
  3. Use LibreOffice to format your finished calculations(Figure 2 Below)

    Calc can create graphs, tables, and charts!

  4. Add more spreadsheets, creating a workbook, using the green plus sign in the bottom left corner of the application
  5. Save your work by clicking 'File' then 'Save As' or by holding 'Ctrl' and 's' simultaneously

    Be sure to frequently save your spreadsheet to avoid lost of information

  6. Print your spreadsheet by clicking 'File' then 'Print' or by holding 'Ctrl' and 'p' simultaneously

    You can also export your spreadsheet to a PDF and print your spreadsheet by pressing the PDF icon or the print icon, respectively

Please use the navigation bar (left-side of the screen) to browse the website.
